This website provides access to protein complexes and stable protein interactions from A pan-plant protein complex map reveals deep conservation and alternate assemblies.

Try "Search complexes" find if your protein is involved in a stable protein complexes. Try "Get top interactions" to see a list of proteins that have similar co-purification to your protein, indicative of stable protein interactions.
Note: the protein complexes and interactions reported here were stable enough to survive biochemical fractionation. More transient interactions (e.g. signalling interactions) are less likely to be observed in these data.

In "Get top interactions", CF-MS scores > 0.5 correspond to ~90% true positive rate; scores > 0.2 correspond to ~50% true positive rate.

  • Uniprot Entry name Entry name ex. PFD4_ARATH
  • TAIR Locus identifier ex. AT1G02090
  • Arabidopsis thaliana gene name ex. COG4
  • Rice locus ex. Os04g0551800
OrthogroupIDs are eggNOG plant orthogroups ex. ENOG411DSAC ENOG411DWGM or ENOG411DWUV
